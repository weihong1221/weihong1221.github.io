TWICE

Twice is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2015 reality show Sixteen. The groupofficially debuted on October 20, 2015, with nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun,Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. TWICE quickly rose to fame with their catchy songs, synchronized choreography, and bright, energetic image. Their hit singles like Cheer Up, TT, Likey, and Feel Special became massive successes, helping them gain both domestic and international popularity. Known as one of the leading K-pop girl groups of the third generation, TWICE has achieved numerous awards and records, including topping charts in South Korea and Japan. Over time, their music has evolved to show a more mature and confident side while maintaining their signature charm.
